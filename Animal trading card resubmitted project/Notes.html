Owl

Owls can turn their heads 135 degrees in either direction

  • Scientific Name: Strigiformes
  • Average Wingspan: 56 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 9 to 10 years
  • Living Environment: Variety of places, including forests, mountains, deserts and plains.

Facial Disk: Owls have a distinct facial structure that resembles a disk. This disk-like face of theirs allows them to amplify sounds in whatever direction in turns towards. This amazingly designed characteristic is just one reason why they are such efficient hunters.
